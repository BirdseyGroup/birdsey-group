# Editing Team Bios on the New Birdsey Group Website

This guide walks you through adding and editing team member bios on the new site, start to finish. No technical background needed; everything happens in a friendly editing screen in your web browser.

## 1. What You'll Need

- **The editor address:** https://birdsey-group-staging.vercel.app/admin
- **Your login.** You already have an editor account (the same one you've used before). If the login screen appears, sign in and you'll land in the content editor.

Note: the site's address will change when we launch on birdseygroup.com. The editing steps will stay exactly the same.

## 2. Open the Team Members List

1. Go to the editor address above and sign in.
2. In the left sidebar, click **Team Members**.
3. You'll see the full list of people on the site. Click any name to open that person's profile for editing.

## 3. Edit a Person's Profile

Each profile has the following fields. You only need to touch the ones you're updating; the rest can stay as they are.

| Field | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Name** | The person's name as shown on their card |
| **Job Title** | Shown under the name |
| **Team Group** | Which tab of the About page they appear under (Corporate, Commercial, Residential, Construction, Board of Advisors) |
| **Photo** | Their headshot (see photo tips below) |
| **Biography** | The full bio text; this is the main thing you'll be writing |
| **Email / Phone / LinkedIn URL** | Contact details on the card |
| **Mini bio enabled** | Turns on the "Mini bio" link on their card. Leave this OFF until their bio is written and approved |
| **Dedicated profile page** | Publishes a full page for the person at birdsey site /team/their-name. Leave OFF unless we've decided they get one |
| **Display Order** | Controls where they appear in their group; lower numbers come first |

### Writing the Biography

The Biography box works like a simple Word document:

1. Click into the **Biography** field.
2. Type or paste the bio text. If you paste from Word, the formatting (paragraphs, bold, italics) comes along.
3. Use the small toolbar above the field for **bold**, *italics*, and paragraph breaks. Keep it simple; the site's design takes care of how it looks.

### Photo Tips

- Photos display as a **square**, so a straight-on headshot with some space around the face crops best.
- Use a photo at least **800 pixels wide**. Bigger is fine; the site resizes automatically.
- To change a photo, click the Photo field, then **upload** a new image from your computer.

## 4. Save Your Work

1. Click the blue **Save** button at the top of the form.
2. That's it. Your change is recorded immediately and the public site updates itself within about five minutes.

If Save is grayed out, a required field is empty. Scroll up and look for a field outlined in red, fill it in, and Save again.

## 5. Check It on the Site

1. Go to https://birdsey-group-staging.vercel.app/about
2. Click the tab for the person's Team Group.
3. Find their card. If "Mini bio enabled" is on, click **Mini bio** to read the bio exactly as visitors will see it.

Allow up to five minutes after saving for changes to appear, and refresh the page.

## 6. Suggested Approval Flow

Since Birdsey needs to approve all bios before launch:

1. Enter all the bios first, leaving **Mini bio enabled** OFF while you work.
2. When a batch is ready, turn **Mini bio enabled** ON for those people.
3. Send Birdsey the About page link (step 5 above) and ask them to click through each Mini bio.
4. Make any requested edits the same way; each Save updates the site within a few minutes.

## 7. Adding a Brand-New Team Member

1. In the sidebar, click **Team Members**, then the **Create New** button (top right).
2. Fill in at least: Name, Job Title, Team Group, Biography, and Display Order.
3. Click **Save**. They'll appear on the About page under their group within a few minutes.
